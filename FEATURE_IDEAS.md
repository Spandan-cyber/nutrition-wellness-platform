# Feature Ideas for Nutrition & Wellness Platform

## 🎯 Quick Win Features (Easy to Implement)

### 1. **Delete Food Log** ⭐⭐⭐
- Add delete button on each food log
- Confirm before deleting
- Update totals instantly
- **Time**: 15 minutes

### 2. **Edit Food Log** ⭐⭐⭐
- Click to edit existing log
- Update values
- Save changes
- **Time**: 20 minutes

### 3. **Food Search/Autocomplete** ⭐⭐
- Search common foods
- Show suggestions (Chicken, Rice, Eggs, etc.)
- Auto-fill calories/macros
- **Time**: 30 minutes

### 4. **Quick Add Buttons** ⭐⭐
- Pre-made food buttons (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)
- Common meals (Chicken + Rice, Salad, etc.)
- One-click logging
- **Time**: 20 minutes

### 5. **Daily Summary Email** ⭐⭐
- Send email at end of day
- Show total calories/macros
- Show progress vs goals
- **Time**: 25 minutes

### 6. **Meal Categories** ⭐⭐
- Tag logs as Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner/Snack
- Filter by meal type
- Show breakdown by meal
- **Time**: 20 minutes

### 7. **Water Intake Tracker** ⭐⭐
- Track glasses of water
- Daily goal (8 glasses)
- Progress bar
- **Time**: 15 minutes

### 8. **Calorie Goal Alerts** ⭐⭐
- Alert when over/under goal
- Show remaining calories
- Color-coded (red/yellow/green)
- **Time**: 15 minutes
